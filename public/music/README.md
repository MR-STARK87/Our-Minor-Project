## Quick Start

Place your MP3 files in this folder to play them on the DEN page music player!

## Instructions

1. **Add Your Music Files**
   - Copy your MP3, WAV, or OGG files into this folder
   - File names will become track titles
   - Files are automatically detected

2. **Access the Music Player**
   - Navigate to the DEN page in your app
   - Click the "Music" pill button at the bottom
   - Your playlist will load automatically

3. **Enjoy!**
   - Click any track to play it
   - Use the controls to play, pause, skip
   - Adjust volume with the slider

## File Naming Tips

- Use descriptive names: `song-title.mp3`
- Avoid special characters
- Use hyphens or underscores instead of spaces
- Example: `lofi-beats.mp3`, `jazz_music.mp3`

## Notes

- Files are automatically sorted alphabetically
- Incomplete downloads (`.crdownload`) are ignored
- Hidden files (starting with `.`) are filtered out
- No file size limit, but keep files reasonable for streaming
